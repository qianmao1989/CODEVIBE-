# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors and maintainers of **CodeVibe** pledge to make participation
in our project and community a welcoming, respectful and inspiring experience for everyone.

We are committed to treating all participants with dignity, regardless of age, body size,
visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression,
level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race,
caste, color, religion or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act in ways that contribute to an open, inclusive and healthy community.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment:

- **Be respectful and inclusive** — Use welcoming and inclusive language. Respect differing
  viewpoints and experiences.
- **Welcome feedback and criticism gracefully** — Accept constructive feedback with an open
  mind. Disagreement is fine; disrespect is not.
- **Focus on what is best for the community** — Prioritize the collective well-being of the
  project and its contributors over individual preferences.
- **Show empathy towards others** — Be kind and patient, especially with new or less
  experienced contributors.
- **Give credit where it is due** — Acknowledge the work and contributions of others.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Use of sexualized language or imagery, or unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting, derogatory comments, or personal/political attacks
- Public or private harassment of any kind
- Publishing others' private information (e.g., physical or email address) without
  explicit permission
- Dismissing or belittling contributions from other community members
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards of acceptable
behavior. They will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior
that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive or harmful.

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit or reject comments, commits,
code, wiki edits, issues and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of
Conduct and will communicate reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — including the GitHub repository,
issue tracker, pull requests, discussions and any other official CodeVibe channels.

It also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces
(e.g., using an official project email address, posting via an official social media account,
or acting as an appointed representative at an online or offline event).

---

## Enforcement

### Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by
contacting the project maintainer directly:

**Jiya Batra** - [GitHub Profile](https://github.com/JiyaBatra)

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The project team is
obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.

### Consequences

Project maintainers will follow these steps when responding to violations:

| Severity | Action |
|----------|--------|
| **Minor** (e.g., use of inappropriate language) | Private written warning with clarity on why the behavior was a violation |
| **Moderate** (e.g., repeated minor violations, harassment) | Temporary suspension from community spaces and project contributions |
| **Severe** (e.g., targeted harassment, discrimination) | Permanent removal from the community and all project spaces |
